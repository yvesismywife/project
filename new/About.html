Why and How Gaming Has Helped Me

Why Gaming Has Helped Me

Gaming has offered me an escape from stress, helping me unwind after challenging days. It's also enhanced my critical thinking and problem-solving skills in ways I never expected.

How Gaming Has Helped Me

Through gaming, I've built friendships some stayed and some didn't but people always come and go, though those who stay are the ones who you truly need and with gaming I also learned teamwork through multiplayer experiences. Certain games have even taught me perseverance and patience, lessons I've carried into daily life.
